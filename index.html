What counts as a "sourced candidate" in this KPI
A candidate is counted as sourced regardless of how they entered the pipeline — LinkedIn, referral, outreach, or inbound application — as long as a TA team member made a deliberate decision to match them to a specific open role.
Counts as sourced
LinkedIn outreach → matched
Referral → reviewed → matched
Inbound → TA reviewed → matched to role
Does not count
Inbound sitting unreviewed
Auto-rejected by system
Reviewed but no role found
% sourced candidates time-in-pipeline

Post-acceptance withdrawal rate
Tracks how many candidates withdrew after formally accepting an offer, before their start date.
Formula: Withdrawals after acceptance ÷ Total accepted offers × 100  |  Target: ≤5%
What counts: Candidate formally accepted (signed or verbally confirmed) but pulled out before first day.
Does not count: Offer declines before acceptance · Rescinded offers by ManifestGlobal.
Reporting period: Use the quarter the offer was accepted, not the quarter of withdrawal.

Definitions & Calculations Guide
Reference this page to understand what each metric means, how it is calculated, and how to pull the data.
1
Pipeline Candidate Diversity
Target ≥ 35–40% per dept
Definition: The % of candidates at each hiring stage who self-declare as belonging to an underrepresented group.

Diverse = either Gender (woman/non-binary in underrepresented dept) OR Ethnicity (underrepresented group). Meeting one dimension is enough.

Never infer from names or photos. Self-declaration only. Exclude "prefer not to say" from both counts.
Formula
Score = (Declared diverse ÷ Answered self-ID) × 100
Example: 28 diverse out of 80 responded = 35%
1
Greenhouse → Reports → Diversity → Pipeline by stage. Filter by quarter.
2
Export CSV. Remove blank and "prefer not to say" rows per department.
3
Count rows where gender = Woman/Non-binary OR ethnicity = underrepresented. Divide by total who answered × 100.
2
Funnel Drop-off
Diagnostic — no single target
Score = (Diverse at stage ÷ Total at stage) × 100
A drop of ≥ 8pts between any two stages = bias risk flag.
3
Interview Panel Diversity
Target ≥ 70%
Score = (Qualifying panels ÷ Total panels) × 100
Qualifying = HM present + at least 1 cross-dept interviewer. TA/HR do not count as cross-dept.
4
Pipeline Coverage Ratio
Target ≥ 3×
Ratio = Total active candidates ÷ Total open roles
Example: 90 candidates, 25 roles = 3.6× ✓. Below 3× = pipeline is thin.
5
% Sourced Candidates Time-in-Pipeline
Target ≥ 60% — Monthly
Score = (Active ≥ 1 month ÷ Total sourced last month) × 100
Counts both proactive sourcing AND inbound applicants TA matched to a role. Active = not terminal stage + Greenhouse activity within 30 days.
6
Post-Acceptance Withdrawal Rate
Target ≤ 5%
Rate = (Withdrawals after acceptance ÷ Total accepted offers) × 100
Count only candidate-initiated withdrawals. Rescinded offers by ManifestGlobal excluded.
1
Filter Greenhouse by Offer Accepted for the quarter. Count total — this is your denominator.
2
Count those who later withdrew or did not start. This is your numerator.
Company quarter calendar
Q1 — July, August, September
Q2 — October, November, December
Q3 — January, February, March
Q4 — April, May, June
Golden rules
1. Denominator = responded only. Never total applicants.
2. Self-declared only — never infer from names or photos.
3. Measure each stage separately.
4. Fewer than 20 responses = record "insufficient data".
5. Don't change definitions mid-cycle.
6. Sourced only for Time-in-Pipeline — no unreviewed inbound.
